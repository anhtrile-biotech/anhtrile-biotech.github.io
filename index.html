Academic Research & Projects

Genetic Polymorphism Analysis

PRL gene mutation | PCR | RFLP analysis | restriction enzyme

Investigated Prolactin (PRL) gene mutations (PRL24 indel and PRL5 C-2402T SNP) in Ri chickens to identify genetic markers associated with egg production and broodiness. Performed PCR amplification and RFLP analysis using AluI restriction enzyme to genotype the PRL5 locus, followed by agarose gel electrophoresis to verify product sizes. Monitored and recorded experimental data while ensuring strict adherence to laboratory protocols and biosafety standards.

Microbial Cultivation & Resistance

transformation | plasmid extraction | bacterial culture

Characterized multi-drug resistance in Salmonella enterica serotype Kentucky 1066 using Whole Genome Sequencing (WGS) and disc diffusion assays. Conducted plasmid extraction and transformation into E. coli JM109 to identify antibiotic resistance genes (ARGs) and evaluate the horizontal transfer of resistance for five major antibiotics. Analyzed zones of inhibition and genomic data to confirm the presence of resistance factors such as blaCTX-M, tet(A), and mutations in gyrA/gyrB.
